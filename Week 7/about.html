Miles Morales was created in 2011 by Brian Michael Bendis and Sara Pichelli, with input from Marvel's then-editor-in-chief Axel Alonso. The release of Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse in 2018 grossed $190.2 million in the United States and Canada, and $185.3 million in other territories, for a total worldwide gross of $375.5 million, against a production budget of $90 million. Upon its release on November 12th, Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales also went on to become the #1 PS5 launch title
